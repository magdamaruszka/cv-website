Laurenz Berges Cloppenburg

1989–1990

Laurenz Berges’s photographs exemplify notions of memory and place, the “that-has-been” about which Roland Barthes spoke so eloquently in his seminal text on photography, Camera Lucida (1980), as well as the mainstays that account for our continued fascination with the photographic medium at a time when it is being threatened by new digital technologies.

Jacques Tati

Trafic, 1971

Trafic (Traffic) is a 1971 Italian-French comedy film directed by Jacques Tati. Trafic was the last film to feature Tati's famous character of Monsieur Hulot, and followed the vein of earlier Tati films that lampooned modern society.
